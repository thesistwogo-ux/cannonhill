How to play

  • ◀ ▶ — swing your cannon left / right.
  • FIRE — hold to charge power, release to shoot. Longer hold = more power.
  • ‹ › — switch weapons. Each has its own ammo and effect.
  • The ground is fully destructible and collapses — bury foes or dig yourself a trench.
  • Mind the wind arrow up top; it pushes shots in flight.
  • Reduce an enemy to 0 health to destroy them. Last tank standing wins the round.
  • Earn cash for damage dealt, then spend it in the shop between rounds.
